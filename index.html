FAQ

Why December 4th? If I forget, can I do this another day?

It is supposed to be ONE day, seen on places all over the world.

But - if you couldn't finish, it's not a huge problem to add a little something later

When is the last day to send the video?

We need the video(s) as soon as possible, December 5 would be great, because we have to edit a lot (a lot!!!)! We have a final presentation at the university in December, and we have to finish everything until then.

I already filmed most of my routine, do I have to completely re-do this?

It would be great if you could add a newspaper from December 4, everything else should be fine.

What is really important to show ? (breakfast, walk, work time, etc.)

Whatever you like, but a landmark of your place and something outside of your flat would be nice.

Why should I send in something? What is the benefit for me?

1. You will be part of a great design project: this is the world on December 4, 2020.

2. You will get a movie with people from everywhere and yourself in a similar situation.

3. you are helping some nice design students to finish a project, get their grades and become famous designer.

How do I upload my files?

When you put your videos (and a text file with your information) on wetransfer, all files will be packed in one folder automatically. We can download them without problems.

You can use the upload button on our website.

Why do you need my data (name, age, occupation, etc.)

All data you give us will only be used to create a video that is worth seeing and interesting. If you want us to delete your data / video, just write us!

What happens with my data?

We want to make it a video of people, and people have faces and names. If you don't want to give us all information, it's fine, too.

When can I see the final video?

The final video will be published on our website and perhaps on youtube, vimeo etc.

What is about privacy? Can everyone later see me, my name and everything forever?

You can decide which information you want to give. You don't have to show your face or give away too much personal information. But this is an international video project, made by design students, to show a certain moment in time all over the world, and of course people would like to see this video. So, it will be published.

If you really don't like this, you shouldn't participate ...

What video format can I use?

We can handle nearly every video format. Please don't make it longer 10 minutes altogether ...

What if my file is too big for WeTransfer?

If your file is too big, its's too long either. Please don't send us more than 10 minutes altogether.

Can I ask friends to join in?

YES
